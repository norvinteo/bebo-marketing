About Bebo Studio

Our story of making pretty things work.

2010

A Bold Beginning

In Singapore, 2010, Bebo Studio was born with a simple yet powerful philosophy: "We make pretty things." The name "Bebo" embodied our founding principle—to "Be Bold" in everything we create. This boldness wasn't about flashy designs or attention-grabbing visuals; it was about having the courage to craft designs that truly represented our clients' essence, even when that meant embracing simplicity and subtlety.

This bold approach to design attracted clients who were looking for visual identities that stood out in Singapore's competitive business landscape. Our founders envisioned a studio that would challenge the status quo, pushing beyond safe design choices to create work that was both beautiful and meaningful.

2015

From Bold Design to Bold Systems

As we immersed ourselves in our clients' businesses to create meaningful designs and branding strategies, we noticed a recurring pattern. While our visual solutions helped businesses project the right image, many struggled with systems and processes that didn't match their external promise. The beautiful websites we designed often connected to inefficient backend processes. The striking brand identities we created sometimes masked operational challenges.

This realization sparked a transformation. We began asking deeper questions about how our "Be Bold" philosophy could extend beyond visuals to improve the systems that power businesses. True boldness, we realized, meant having the courage to address both the visible and invisible aspects of a business.

By 2015, we had expanded our team to include developers who shared our commitment to bold, elegant, functional solutions. Our tagline evolved from merely "making pretty things" to "making pretty things work"—a subtle but significant shift that reflects our commitment to creating solutions that are both visually stunning and functionally powerful.

Today

Our Approach: Bold Thinking for Complete Solutions

Today, Bebo Studio stands at the intersection of design excellence and systems innovation. Our "Be Bold" philosophy infuses everything we do, leading us to create solutions that:

  • Boldly capture attention – with visuals and branding that communicate values instantly and memorably
  • Boldly simplify complexity – creating intuitive experiences that feel natural and effortless
  • Boldly integrate intelligence – building behind-the-scenes infrastructure that scales and adapts

Our integrated approach means we don't just create brands—we build complete brand systems that transform how businesses connect with their audiences. We don't just design websites—we boldly build web applications that transform business operations. We don't just design interfaces—we boldly architect mobile ecosystems that connect businesses with their customers.
